My Story

FlightPlus was born from a simple yet profound love for the skies—where aviation meets technology, and where every journey becomes a story worth preserving.

I am Victor, a FAA Private Pilot with a deep passion for aviation that extends far beyond the cockpit. My day job is as a data scientist at a major technology company in Singapore, where I use AI to build consumer products. I own a Diamond DA 40 NG aircraft, which I fly for leisure and where I continue to develop my flying skills. My true calling lies at the intersection of these worlds: I want to be an AI builder, creating innovative tools that transform how aviators experience and document their journeys through the skies.

My first cross-country solo on Cessna 172

FlightPlus is my answer to a problem I've encountered time and again during my own flights. As someone who loves photography and captures countless moments during my trips and flights, I've always been captivated by the breathtaking views from above—the way buildings, landmarks, mountains, and rivers unfold beneath the wings. There's something magical about that perspective, about looking down and seeing the world from a completely different angle. I wanted to capture these moments, to preserve them, and to understand what I was seeing.

Yet, despite my passion for both flying and photography, I found that no existing tool could help me organize photos from flight trips, identify the interesting landmarks in my aerial shots, or tell me where I was flying through at any given moment. The tools available were either too generic or didn't understand the unique context of aviation. That gap between what I needed and what existed became the inspiration for FlightPlus.

Flying with my Diamond DA 40 NG

My vision for FlightPlus is to enrich the flight journey for aviators—not just to help with the technical aspects of flying, but to enhance the experience of enjoying the beautiful scenery from the sky. I believe that every flight tells a story, and every aerial photo captures a moment that deserves to be understood, organized, and shared. FlightPlus is designed as an AI companion app for pilots and frequent flyers, helping you discover landmarks, organize your aerial memories, and share your journey with fellow aviation enthusiasts.

Through FlightPlus, I want to share my passion for photography and aviation with friends and fellow pilots. I'm using AI tools to build something meaningful—a platform that helps us discover landmarks, curate beautiful aerial photos, and share our flight journeys in ways that were never possible before. This isn't just an app; it's a new sky canvas for every aviator to share their memories and discover their next journey. I envision FlightPlus becoming a hub for the aviation community—a place where pilots and aviation enthusiasts connect, share, and inspire each other.

As I continue to build and iterate on FlightPlus, I'm always looking for feedback and ideas from fellow aviators. Your insights help me understand what features truly matter, what problems need solving, and how we can make this product genuinely useful for every pilot and aviation enthusiast. Whether you're a private pilot like me, a commercial aviator, or simply someone who loves the view from above, I want FlightPlus to become an essential companion for your aerial adventures.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Together, let's make every flight a story worth telling.
