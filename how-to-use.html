How to Use - Upload & View Your Aerial Photos

Follow these simple steps to upload your aerial photos, discover their content, and view them on the map. FlightPlus automatically analyzes your photos and attaches detailed flight information.

1

Enter Flight Number

At the plus page, enter your flight number (tail number) and tap "Set" button in the input field. Then click "View Phone Gallery" to select an aerial photo from your phone album.

2

Analyzing Aerial Photo

If the flight number is correct and the photo timestamp matches when it was captured, the app will automatically analyze the photo content and attach detailed flight information.

3

Discover Flight Details

Specify the camera view angle for your photo. Discover all detailed flight information including date, time, altitude, speed, track, and more. Check the embedded map view to see exactly where the photo was taken.

4

Create Map-Based Frame

You can create a map-based frame for your photo by selecting a template style (Landscape or Portrait). The frame combines your photo with the flight route map and flight data, perfect for sharing with friends.

5

Publish or Keep Private

Click "Publish" to allow other users to view your aerial photos on the map. Alternatively, click the yellow lock icon on the photo preview to set it as private. The "Publish" button will change to "Keep" so only you can see the photo.

6

Explore Photo on Map

After clicking "Publish" or "Keep", your aerial photo will appear on the map page. Click the arrow to center the photo on the map and explore the surrounding area. You can also use the filter buttons "ALL", "MY PHOTOS" or adjust the flight altitude indicator to view more photos.

7

View Photos in Flight Album

At your profile page, you can view the flight album for a specific flight. Tap any photo in the album to see the detailed flight information including altitude, track, speed, and location description.

8

Track Photos Along Flight Route

View all your photos pinned along the flight route on the Flight Track screen. See where each photo was taken during your journey and explore the complete visual story of your flight.

9

Enjoy Your Flight Photo Collection

All your public and private photos are shown in the photo gallery on your profile page. Photos marked with a yellow lock icon are private and visible only to you, while others are public and visible to everyone.
